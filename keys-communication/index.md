## Overview

Keys Communication is an app developed by Xi Cheng / Juggle. This privacy policy describes our current data practices. Because Keys Communication is actively developed, this policy will be updated to reflect any changes before each release.

---

## Data We Collect

At this time, Keys Communication does not collect any personal data. The app does not:

- Collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, camera, or microphone beyond features you explicitly use
- Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
- Transmit data to external servers

All data is stored locally on your device.

---

## Communication Data

As a communication-focused app, Keys Communication may process message content locally on your device to deliver its core functionality. This content is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is not stored outside your device.

---

## Children's Privacy

Keys Communication does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Contact [hellojuggler.dev@gmail.com](mailto:hellojuggler.dev@gmail.com) if you have concerns.

---

## Third-Party Services

Keys Communication does not currently integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. Any future integrations will be disclosed here before they are enabled.

---

## Data Retention

No personal data is collected or stored on external servers. Data on your device is managed by your device's operating system and app storage settings.
