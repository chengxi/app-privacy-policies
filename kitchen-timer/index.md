## Overview

Kitchen Timer is a simple, offline cooking timer app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is (and is not) collected when you use Kitchen Timer.

---

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Kitchen Timer operates entirely on your device. It does not:

- Collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or photos
- Connect to the internet
- Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
- Store any data on external servers

All timer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

---

## Children's Privacy

Kitchen Timer does not collect data from anyone, including children under 16. It is safe for use by children.

---

## Third-Party Services

Kitchen Timer does not integrate any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

---

## Data Retention

Since no data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete.

## Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you have any questions, contact us at [hellojuggler.dev@gmail.com](mailto:hellojuggler.dev@gmail.com).

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in future app versions, we will update the **Last Updated** date above and, where required, notify you through the app or app store release notes.
